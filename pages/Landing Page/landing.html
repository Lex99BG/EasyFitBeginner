What is Fitness

01.

Cardiorespiratory Endurance

Cardiorespiratory endurance, also known as cardiovascular or aerobic endurance, refers to the ability of the circulatory and respiratory systems to supply oxygen and nutrients to the muscles during sustained physical activity. It involves the efficient functioning of the heart, lungs, blood vessels, and the transportation of oxygen throughout the body.
